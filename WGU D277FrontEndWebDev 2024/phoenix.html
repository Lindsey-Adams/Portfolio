About Phoenix

Phoenix is the capitol city of Arizona and is located in Maricopa county. It is home to Arizona State University, the Phoenix Suns, Arizona Diamondbacks, and the Arizona Cardinals. You can learn more by visiting the City of Phoenix website. Phoenix is the most populous city in Arizona, and in the top ten states with the fastest growth in the US. Phoenix is also known as the Valley of the Sun. Phoenix economy includes a variety of industries, such as technology/aerospace, manufacturing, real estate, finance/insurance, and health care. Phoenix locals and tourist enjoy a wide variety of attractions including concerts, high end spas and resorts, golfing, food and cultural sites, and outdoor recreation.

Native Tribes

Phoenix is culturally defined by its current and historical people. The Gila River Indian Community is located in and just south of the Phoenix metro. It is home for the Pima and Maricopa Tribes. You can learn more by visiting the My Gila River website. Maricopa county is also home to the Salt River Pima-Maricopa Indian community, the Fort McDowell Yavapai Nation, and the Tohono O'odham Nation (Wikipedia Contributors, 2023). The Gila River Indian Community continue to be a popular local and tourist destination for their HuHugam Heritage Center, Gila River Hotels & Casino locations, and their Toka Sticks Golf Club.
